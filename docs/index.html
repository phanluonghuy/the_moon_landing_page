Get to Know Me

Introduce About Myself

My name is Nguyen Thi Nhu Nguyet. Born in 2003, I graduated with a degree in Event Management from the University of Finance and Marketing.

With more than two years of experience in the event industry, I have worked primarily in Account and Project Management roles, serving as the key liaison between clients and internal teams to ensure seamless project execution and effective collaboration.

One of my greatest achievements is staying committed to my passion for the event industry even after graduation. I continue to pursue this path with dedication, resilience, and enthusiasm, always striving to deliver impactful experiences and grow further within the field.

Personal Goals

To me, events are more than just grand stages enhanced by modern technology. An event is a platform where brands can express their values, identity, and unique personality through visual experiences, sound, lighting, and technology.

Career Orientation

I aspire to work in a professional environment where I can learn from experienced industry leaders while continuously enhancing essential soft skills such as time management, crisis handling, and creative thinking.
